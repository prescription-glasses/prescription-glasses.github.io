Demystifying Your Eye Prescription: A Crucial Skill for Online Shoppers

Decoding the Key Abbreviations and Numbers

SPH (Spherical Correction): Lens strength for farsightedness (+) or nearsightedness (-).

CYL (Cylindrical Correction): Amount of astigmatism; left blank if none.

AXIS: Location of astigmatism, represented by a number between 1 and 180 degrees.

ADD: Additional lens power for reading, used for separate reading glasses or bifocals.

OD (Oculus Dexter) & OS (Oculus Sinister): Latin for right eye and left eye, respectively. (Source: American Academy of Ophthalmology, CNET)
