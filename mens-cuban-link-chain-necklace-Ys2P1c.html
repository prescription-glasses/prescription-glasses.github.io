Measuring Your Pupillary Distance (PD)

    
Your Pupillary Distance (PD) is a critical measurement that dictates where the optical center of your lenses should be placed. It’s the distance between the centers of your pupils. An incorrect PD can lead to prism effect, causing eye strain, headaches, and even distorted vision, which is particularly detrimental in sports where precise visual input is paramount.

    
For the most accurate PD measurement, it is best obtained from your optometrist during your eye exam. However, if this isn't possible, various online tools and DIY methods can provide an estimate. Keep in mind that for **custom prescription sports glasses** and high-wrap frames, precision in PD is even more critical to prevent discomfort and ensure clear, unhindered vision across your entire field of view.
